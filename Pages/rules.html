  • Make sure the group in front of you is out of range before taking your shot and, if there is a blind sight, verify if it’s clear.

  • Play fast and quit de green as soon as you can.

  • Note your score at the next hole.

  • Replace grass mounds, repair ball marks, rake the sand traps.

  • Don’t walk in the putting line of your partners.

  • Roll the push carts around and behind the green.

  • The carts have to stay on the cart paths near the greens; drive safely at all time, especially when the course if humid, and follow the daily indications.

  • Have a respectful behavior at all time.

  • When a player is about to shoot: stay quiet, be calm, position yourself far enough from the player and stay out of his field of view.

  • Always prepare yourself for your next shot, especially on the greens.

Respect the Rules

Members have to respect and make their guests respect the rules of the Balmoral Golf Club and to adopt a reasonable behavior on the premises of the Club. Disrespectful members will have the privileges suspended or cancelled.
