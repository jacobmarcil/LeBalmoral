Le Champagne welcomes you

Le Champagne, open all year long, compares in taste and finesse to te best tables in Quebec. Its chef Olivier Côté and his team make sure to provide you with the service an exceptional golf club deserves, from breakfast to dinner.

You will discover the totally revisited "Lounge" menu, the best option for your 4 à 7, and a meeting with great cuisine. The 9 ½ also offers a selection of sandwiches and snacks for a quick bite.

Start well, or end the day on a good note

A unique breakfast will be offered from Monday to Thursday from 8:30 am, and our traditional breakfast menu shines from Friday to Sunday.

A "table d'hôte" menu is also offered during the day and at night, Le Champagne presents a gastronomic menu for which it is renowned. Also enjoy the Pasta Bar, our Sunday night tradition.

A personalized cuisine concept adapted to your taste is offered on reservation for business meals, or a get together with friends, and served in the comfort of our terrace or in our dining room.

Our AQAA certification (Association Québécoise des Allergies Alimentaires) will allow you to enjoy your meal in total confidence.
