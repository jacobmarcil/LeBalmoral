The Balmoral Pro Shop offers its members a vast selection of high end items. The instauration of the Mill River System in 1998 has contributed to the success of our Pro Shop. Well known by the Quebec golfers, Mill River allows to maintain an always renewed inventory of golf clothing and accessories from the top brands.

Members benefit from an important discount on the retail price. They pay the cost plus 25% on clothes, 20% on bags, shoes and accessories and 15% on golf clubs. An annual contribution of 70,00$ for the principal member and 70,00$ for Spouse / Associate members will be billed at the beginning of the season.
