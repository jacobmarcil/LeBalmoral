Martin Plante

Class A golf pro in the CPGA and member since 1989, golf does not hold a lot of secrets for Martin Plante. As Assistant-Pro at Lorraine and Le Blainvillier Golf Club and Pro at Laval-Sur-Le-Lac and Le Challenger Golf Club, Martin has developed a real talent for golf coaching. He is also a born competitor, as he won the Player of the Year award on the Honda Tour in 2008, as well as the Quebec Skins Game 2008 and the Honda Classic AGP in 2006.

Jean Savard

Golf Pro for 30 years and retired elite hockey player, Jean Savard is a born passionate. While he played for the Chicago Blackhawks in the NHL, Jean became a member of the CPGA in 1977. After concluding a great hockey career, he came back to his love for golf and became Pro for La Seigneurie, L’Atlantide and Napierville. Since 2006, he has occupied the role of General Manager of Balmoral Golf Club. His door is always open and he will be happy to help you, either with your membership or with your swing.

Come meet our Pros. They will be happy to share their experience and to guide you to the expected results.
