Laurentides’ Treasure

Located in Morin-Heights in Saint-Sauveur’s valley in the Laurentides, the 18-hole the Balmoral Golf Club offers you a golf resort that will exceed your expectations. Recognized for the quality of the course, it also represents a real challenge for golfers of every level, from amateur to expert. The Balmoral Golf Club is a course like we do not see everyday, as it seems the valley of Saint-Sauveur / Morin-Heights was conceived to be home of such a wonder. Come meet our Pros, they will be happy to share their experience with you and to guide you to the desired results. The Balmoral Pros are always available to our members and their guests for private or group lessons.
